If you are in a school where Tor is blocked, you can configure Tor to connect to a bridge during the setup process. Thanks to the "Great Firewall of LAUSD", Tor is censored in school. Switch the bridge to either "obsv4" or "meek-azure" to connect.

Stay safe

We do not recommend installing additional add-ons or plugins into Tor Browser. Tor Browser will block browser plugins such as Flash, RealPlayer, QuickTime, and others: they can be manipulated into revealing your IP address.
